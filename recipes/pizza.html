Pizza

Description

This delicious homemade pizza is super easy to make, only requiring a few common ingredients and around 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Instant Yeast
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine instant yeast with luke warm water until activated.
  2. Mix in beaten eggs and flour till dough forms
  3. Roll out dough into desired thickness
  4. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees
  5. Bake crust for 20 minutes or until lightly browned
  6. Add tomato sauce and cheese, along with any other desired toppings
  7. Bake pizza for additional 30 minutes or until done
  8. Remove from oven and allow to cool
